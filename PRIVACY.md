## Overview

Fuzzy History Search is a browser extension that searches your local browsing history using fuzzy matching. Your privacy is important and this extension is designed to operate entirely on your device.

## Data Collection

This extension does **not** collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or browsing information with any external server, third party, or the extension developer.

## Data Usage

- **Browser history** is read locally on your device solely to provide search results within the extension.
- All processing happens entirely within your browser. No data ever leaves your device.
- No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind is implemented.

## Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `history` | Read local browsing history to provide search results |
| `tabs` | Query active tab info and open search results |
| `activeTab` | Inject the overlay search panel into the current page |
| `scripting` | Programmatically inject the overlay script (Manifest V3) |

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs.
